About the Ambrose Alli ETD

The Ambrose Alli ETD (Electronic Theses and Dissertations) is a collection of records of theses and dissertations submitted electronically by students of the university. These digital documents are suitable for machine archives and worldwide access.

In Ambrose Alli University, graduate students began submitting ETDs during the fall 2019.

Increased access to research

Available to potential employers and other researchers from anywhere, at any time.

Cost and space saving

No need to print, copy and bind paper theses. No need for storage space in libraries.
